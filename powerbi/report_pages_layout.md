# Report Pages Layout

## Page 1: Executive Overview

Purpose: leadership summary of consumption, meter operations, and data trust.

Visuals:

- KPI cards: Total Consumption, Active Meters, Reading Success Rate, Missing Reading %, Anomaly Count
- Line chart: monthly consumption trend
- Bar chart: consumption by region
- Table: critical meters with meter ID, region, meter type, health score, freshness hours
- Slicers: date, region, meter type, customer type

## Page 2: Consumption Analysis

Purpose: understand usage patterns by time, segment, meter type, and region.

Visuals:

- Daily or monthly consumption trend
- Stacked bar: consumption by customer type
- Donut or bar chart: consumption by meter type
- Region comparison bar chart
- Top 10 high-consumption meters or customers

## Page 3: Meter Health & Data Quality

Purpose: help operations find meters and regions with reliability problems.

Visuals:

- Bar chart: missing readings by region
- Bar chart: anomaly count by region
- Column chart: health status distribution
- Card or gauge: average data freshness hours
- Table: critical meters with health score, missing 30d, anomaly 30d, freshness hours

## Page 4: Anomaly Detection

Purpose: investigate unusual consumption spikes.

Visuals:

- Line or column chart: anomalies over time
- Table: anomaly details with meter, timestamp, value, customer type, reason
- Bar chart: abnormal meters by count
- Scatter or column chart: spike values by meter type
- Filters: region, meter type, customer type, date

## Page 5: Metric Definitions

Purpose: make the dashboard self-service and reduce metric confusion.

Visuals:

- Table with metric name, definition, formula, business meaning, data source, validation note
- Short explanatory text for Reading Success Rate, Active Meters, Anomaly Count, Data Freshness, and Health Score
