# Business Context

Nordic Utility Insights is a fictional Danish utility technology company that helps energy and water providers monitor smart-meter data. The company serves residential, commercial, and industrial customers across Aarhus, Horsens, Skanderborg, Vejle, Odense, and Copenhagen.

Every day, smart meters send readings for electricity, water, and heat. Business stakeholders need to understand whether consumption is changing, whether meters are reporting reliably, and whether data quality is strong enough to support operational and commercial decisions.

## Stakeholders

- Operations teams need to find delayed, inactive, or unhealthy meters.
- Data and analytics teams need a validated dataset for Power BI reporting.
- Customer and regional managers need consumption trends by segment and geography.
- Leadership needs executive KPIs for total consumption, reading success, missing readings, anomalies, and meter health.

## Business Questions

- How much utility consumption was recorded over time?
- Which regions and customer segments consume the most?
- What percentage of expected readings were successfully received?
- Which meters have missing readings, delayed readings, or abnormal spikes?
- How fresh is the available meter data?
- Which meters require follow-up from operations?

## Value

The platform turns raw smart-meter events into trusted business metrics. It connects engineering, data quality, database design, API delivery, and self-service BI into one realistic analytics workflow.
